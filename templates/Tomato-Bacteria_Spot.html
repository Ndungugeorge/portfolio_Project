Treatment:

Copper fungicides are the most commonly recommended treatment for bacterial leaf spot. Apply copper fungicide as a preventive measure before moving the plants to their permanent location. In case of symptoms, spray with copper fungicide for a 7-10 day period and repeat weekly after transplanting. Continue treatments every 10 days in dry weather and every 5-7 days during rainy weather.
